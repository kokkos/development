This file just lists the official responsibilities that are assigned to this working group

- Direct build systems maintenance of core, kernels, tools while supporting the rest of the ecosystem's build systems
- Help with educating about the build system in coordination with the training wg
- Documentation of build and packaging stuff, especially the `getting started` section and the packaging section
- Maintain the Spack, godbolt, easybuild, Conan recipes to build Kokkos and potential binary packaging
- Provide guidance for the ecosystem on creating recipes
- Ensure that our recipes are tested appropriately
- Disseminate knowledge to reduce our bus factor
- Support/help special cases of build system integration
- Help packaging, especially with configuration options, Kokkos and Kokkos ecosystem for linux distributions
