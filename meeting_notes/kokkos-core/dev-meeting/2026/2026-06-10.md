-   Nick is serving as release manager.

    meeting.

### 128-Bit Atomic Fix

#### Summary

Jakob reported ongoing work to address a bug in the implementation of
128-bit atomics.

Background:

-   Damier produced an initial redesign.
-   Jakob took over implementation and integration work.

#### Impact

The new implementation:

-   Fixes correctness issues in the current atomic implementation.
-   May introduce a performance regression on Hopper GPUs for users
    relying on 128-bit atomics.

Despite the performance impact:

-   The change is considered necessary to resolve the correctness
    problem.

### Key Takeaways

1.  CI reliability remains the primary operational concern, though
    stability is improving.
2.  The July release remains on track with no major blockers identified.
3.  The 128-bit atomic fix is important and likely release-relevant
    despite potential Hopper performance regressions.
4.  Several teams are focused on benchmarking, performance analysis, and
    publication efforts.
5.  Mixed-precision support and type-conversion semantics are emerging
    as ecosystem-wide design concerns.
6.  Most of the meeting consisted of project status updates rather than
    major design decisions.
