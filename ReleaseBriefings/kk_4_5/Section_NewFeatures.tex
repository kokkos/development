\begin{frame}[fragile]{Batched updates}
  Supporting positive definitive symmetric (PDS) banded solvers and preparation for general matrix solvers.\\[1em]
  \textbf{Content:}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Batched serial \texttt{laswp}: Series of row interchanges on the matrix A
        \item Batched serial \texttt{iamax}: Finds the index of the first element with max absolute value
        \item Batched serial \texttt{pbtrs}: Solve PDS banded matrix A factorized by \texttt{pbtrf}
        \item Batched serial \texttt{pbtrf}: Cholesky factorization of PDS banded matrix A
        \item Batched serial \texttt{pttrs}: Solve tridiagonal system A factorized by \texttt{pttrf}
    \end{itemize}

\end{frame}
